Documents for Eligibility

  • A copy of the learner's latest report
  • A copy of both biological parents' identity documents
  • A copy of the death certificate if either parent is deceased.
  • A copy of the learner's identity document and or birth certificate
  • A copy of proof of residential address. N.B. Offer to purchase is not acceptable
  • A copy of the learner's UNABRIDGED CERTIFICATE if biological parents' identity documents are not available
