Who are we?

Happy Home Academy is a nurturing, future-focused school dedicated to helping every child thrive. We combine academic excellence, character development, and community spirit to build confident learners.

Our teachers are committed to delivering inspiring lessons in a supportive environment that encourages creativity, curiosity, and meaningful growth.

Our Mission & Vision

Shaping Tomorrow’s Learners

At the heart of our school lies a clear mission that inspires learning and empowers every student to thrive both inside and beyond the classroom.

Our Mission

We provide a caring and structured learning environment where students gain the knowledge, skills, and confidence to succeed. Every learner receives personalised attention and guidance to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To become a leading community school known for excellence, integrity, and transformational learning. We aim to prepare young people who are curious, capable, and ready for a changing world.
